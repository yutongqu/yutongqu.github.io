## Welcome

---

### About me

I am currently a Ph.D student at the [School of Computer and Mathematical Sciences](https://set.adelaide.edu.au/computer-and-mathematical-sciences/), [Faculty of Sciences, Engineering and Technology](https://set.adelaide.edu.au/), [The University of Adelaide](https://adelaide.edu.au/). I am researching in document summarization under the supervision of [Dr. Wei Zhang](https://weiezhang.github.io/) and co-supervision of Dr. Weitong Chen.


---

### Education

- BCOMP, majoring in Artificial Intelligence, The University of Adelaide, Australia, 2018-2021
